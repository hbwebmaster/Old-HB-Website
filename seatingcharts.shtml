Purpose of Seating Charts

Seating charts are provided by the Honor Board in order to facilitate the requirement that the seating arrangement of students in an exam is recorded. This should not in any way imply that student seating is not at the discretion of the students. Students are permitted, according to the Honor System bylaws, to choose their own seats during an exam. No assigned rows or special arrangement is permitted.
